Terms & Conditions

All participants must be above 18 years of age or accompanied by a guardian. Bookings are confirmed only after full payment is received.

Karnataka Adventures prioritizes safety; however, trekking involves inherent risks. Participants must follow guide instructions at all times.

A valid government-issued ID proof is mandatory. Failure to comply with rules may result in removal without refund.

The organizer reserves the right to modify or cancel the trek due to weather, safety concerns, or unforeseen circumstances.

Cancellation Policy

If cancellation is made 7 or more days before the trek date, 80% of the booking amount will be refunded.

Cancellations made between 3 to 6 days before the trek are eligible for a 50% refund.

No refunds will be provided for cancellations within 3 days or for participants who fail to show up.

Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 working days to the original payment method.
